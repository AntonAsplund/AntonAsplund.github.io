The story of MRELAND©

I've always kept my interest of cooking and outdoor life close, rarely have I been able two combine those two.

During my military service in the swedish army. I got to know the about the world of field rations.

Many countries around the globe source through civilian contractors or state owned company, their own field ration. To provide an easy to come by meal whilst being deployed. Some conutries have taken this litterally and bascially always eats filed rations whilst outside the countries borders. Others have gone set up field kitchens which provide on site cooked food, and whilst deployed in combat zone the troops are being issued high quality meals.

A countries field rations reflects a set menu of the most popular dishes of that country. So while eating field rations from different countries a person might go on a food world tour. Within the luxury of their own home.

We wanted to help people make that journey. Most of the time the field rations are expensive because of many middle mens. So we aim for a procurement from a source as close to the manufacturer as possible.

Our philosophy

To provide the civilian market with afforable MRE's. Always fresh and within date.
